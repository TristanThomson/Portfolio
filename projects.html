"LaciCloud is your open-source private FTP(s)-cloud storage and PHP webhost with many features, including a flexible Bitcoin payment method, user-data encryption, first-class security, compatibility with virtually all computing devices around the world and extreme versatility with an API and user subdomains."

LaciCloud was built from the ground up by a friend of mine and me, self-hosted with self-built servers. While I already knew about hardware and building computers, it was an introduction to webdesign and management, also allowing me to discover the world of e-commerce and cryptocurrencies.

LaciCloud.net

Fablab EEB1 was dreamt of after a group of students in my school were rewarded a 3d-printer for winning a tech-recycling competition. I was part of the first team that imagined the fablab as a learning resource for students to come in contact with industrial design and new technologies.

After a successful crowdfunding campaign and sponsoring from our school, we sarted developing this idea in collaboration with supporting teachers and parents in the industries involved.

Nowadays, Fablab EEB1 has achieved its goal of inspiring students, giving them access to recent technology and supporting their projects.
